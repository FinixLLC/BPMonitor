Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2026

1. Overview

BPMonitor is a patient-facing iOS app for manually logging blood pressure readings and viewing trends over time. It is designed for informational and educational purposes only. The app does not require login or registration. See the Apple Health documentation for more information about integrating with HealthKit.

2. Information Collected

BPMonitor does not collect personal information or send data to any external servers. All blood pressure readings are entered manually by the user and stored locally on the device.

3. Apple Health Integration

With your explicit permission, BPMonitor may write blood pressure data to Apple Health. This integration is optional and fully controlled by you. The app does not read any Apple Health data unless you explicitly enable it.

4. Data Storage and Processing

All data is processed and stored locally on your device. BPMonitor does not operate backend servers, and your data is never transmitted off-device unless you choose to export it manually.

5. Data Sharing

BPMonitor does not share your data with third parties. No data is sold, rented, or used for advertising or tracking.

6. Analytics and Tracking

BPMonitor does not include third-party analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, or tracking technologies.

7. Medical Disclaimer

BPMonitor is not a medical device and is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any medical condition. The app is provided for informational and educational purposes only. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional regarding medical concerns.

8. Children’s Privacy

BPMonitor is not intended for children under 13 years old. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
