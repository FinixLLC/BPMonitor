# BPMonitor

BPMonitor is a **patient-facing iOS app** for manually logging blood pressure readings, viewing trends, and optionally writing data to Apple Health.  
This app is designed for **informational and educational purposes only** and is **not a medical device**.

---

## Features

- Manually log **blood pressure readings** and pulse
- View **daily, weekly, and monthly trends** with charts
- Optional **Apple Health integration** (write-only)
- **No login required** — all data stays on your device
- **Privacy-first**: no third-party tracking or analytics

---

## Privacy Policy

All data is stored **locally on the device**. With your permission, readings may be written to Apple Health.  
For full details, see the hosted Privacy Policy:

[Privacy Policy](https://finixllc.github.io/BPMonitor/privacy/)
